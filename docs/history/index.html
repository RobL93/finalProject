History of Dunshaughlin

Early Christian Roots

Dunshaughlin, known in Irish as Domhnach Seachnaill, traces its origins back to the 5th century when St. Seachnall founded a church here, one of the earliest in Ireland. The town grew around this religious site, which gave it both its name and cultural importance.
